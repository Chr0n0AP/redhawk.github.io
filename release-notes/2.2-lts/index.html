    REDHAWK 2.2

    The REDHAWK Program Office commits to support each Long Term Support (LTS) baseline of the REDHAWK product for four years. An LTS baseline of REDHAWK is defined as: a feature complete series that may include sub-minor version releases consisting of bug fixes only. Both API and ABI forwards/backwards compatibility are guaranteed between sub-minor releases. Long Term Support (LTS) for the REDHAWK 2.2.x releases will continue through September 2022.
