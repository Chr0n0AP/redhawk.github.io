    Using Devices to Interface with FPGAs

    Many Software-Defined Radio (SDR) systems depend on custom hardware solutions implemented on FPGAs and GPUs. REDHAWK has developed a design pattern to interface with these custom hardware solutions that enables users and the REDHAWK Framework alike to change the behavior of an FPGA at run-time to meet the needs of the application. Refer to REDHAWK Persona Device Pattern for more information.
